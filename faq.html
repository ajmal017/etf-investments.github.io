FAQ About ETF.Investments

  • What is ETF.Investments? ETF.Ivestments is a site that monitors and highlights daily ETF Fund Flows
  • What universe do you cover? US ETFs
  • Where do you get your data? ETF fund flows are derived from daily shares outstanding numbers. This is publicly available data that can be found on most ETF issuer's fund page.
  • How timely is your data? We make every effort to get the latest information around fund flows and update the site before the US market open. Intraday pricing data is provided for free by IEX via their API.
  • What is a creation or redemption? Creations and redemptions are the mechanism by which you can trade directly with the ETF Fund. Only Authorized Participants can trade in this way. Monitoring ETF fund flows effectively monitors the net daily creations-redemption activity.
